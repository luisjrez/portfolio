I started as a trainee and grew into a senior engineer by staying curious and shipping real products — from a school-bus tracker in Mexico City to white-label real-estate apps used across the US, Canada and Australia.

Mobile is my home base, but I've never boxed myself in: I move fluidly across mobile, web, backend and databases, and I reach for whatever the problem needs — React Native, NestJS, Python, PostgreSQL or an AI integration. I care about clean architecture, performance where it counts, and pragmatic delivery over gold-plating.

Lately I've been deep into AI-assisted engineering — spec-driven development with tools like Claude Code, Codex and Cursor — folding it into how I build and into the products themselves.
